Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 7, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Suggestory (“we”, “us”, or “our”), a Shopify app, collects, uses, and protects the information you provide when using the application. Your privacy is important to us and we are committed to protecting it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Shop Data: When you install Suggestory, we collect your store name, email address, and shop domain.
  • Order Data: We access and store data about your orders, including product SKUs, quantities, prices, fulfillment status, and customer location data (non-personal).
  • Inventory Data: We collect inventory levels by SKU to calculate replenishment needs.
  • Usage Metrics: We may log feature usage, request counts, and technical errors to improve app performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate daily run rate (DRR) and inventory replenishment suggestions
  • To personalize your dashboard, app settings, and reports
  • To notify you about product changes or critical alerts
  • To comply with Shopify's Partner Program and API terms
  • To improve our app features and customer support

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party. Your data is used solely for the app functionality described above. Access is limited to authorized backend systems hosted securely on AWS.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored on AWS DynamoDB and Amazon S3, encrypted at rest and in transit.
  • We implement access control using environment-managed API tokens.
  • Only internal scripts and AWS Lambda services access your data.
  • Access tokens are stored securely and rotated periodically.

5. Retention and Deletion

  • We retain your data only as long as your app is installed and active.
  • You can uninstall the app anytime to revoke access.
  • Upon uninstallation, we automatically delete or anonymize all data within 14 days unless required for legal or troubleshooting purposes.
  • You may also contact us directly for immediate deletion.

6. Third-Party Services

We use Shopify’s APIs as part of the app's core functionality. We do not integrate with or share data with any external third-party services beyond Shopify and AWS.

7. Your Rights

  • You have the right to access the data we store about your shop
  • You can request deletion or export of your stored data by contacting support
  • You have the right to revoke access by uninstalling the app from your Shopify admin

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may occasionally update this policy to reflect changes in our app or legal obligations. When we do, we will revise the “Effective Date” above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the new policy.
